HabitStreak Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-07 · Last updated: 2026-05-07

The short version

HabitStreak collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, and shows no ads. Everything you record stays on your device. We have no account system, no analytics, no telemetry, and no third-party SDKs.

Information We Collect

We collect nothing. HabitStreak does not request your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

How Your Data Is Stored

Habit names, daily completion records, and streak counts are stored locally on your device using your browser's or system's local storage (a key called habitstreak_data). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to Genesis Forge or any third party
  • Is not backed up to any cloud
  • Will be permanently deleted if you uninstall the app or clear local storage

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data, because we don't collect any.

Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

HabitStreak contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting, and no third-party tracking scripts. The only external resource the web version loads is a Google Fonts stylesheet for typography; no identifying information is sent in this request.

Permissions

The Android version of HabitStreak does not request any device permissions beyond the network access required to load the app shell on first launch. After first launch, the app works fully offline.

Children's Privacy

HabitStreak is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal information from anyone, there is nothing for us to inadvertently collect from a child.

Data Backup and Recovery

Because no data is sent to any server, uninstalling the app or clearing local storage will permanently delete all your habit history. We cannot recover this data for you. If you wish to keep a record, take periodic screenshots.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to genesisforge.contact@gmail.com.
